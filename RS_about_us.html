ABOUT ROHINI SCIENTIFIC

Mission & Vision

ABOUT US

Rohini Scientific is an emerging Indian company, founded in early 2019, with a mission to serve the farming community. At Rohini Scientific, we promote, plan and implement sustainable techniques and methodologies in agriculture, agri-buisness and allied sectors. Our focus is mainly on the issues related to agriculture and aid farmers with respect to improving their agricultural produce by implementing advanced technological innovations. Our solutions are evolved through decades of research experience.Hence, we successfully collate the information and bring optimized practical solutions on one plarform with required modification that suits customer requirements. Our organic farming solutions make farming sustainable and suitable for domestic as well as export purposes.We offer one stop solution for all agriculture related issues from sowing to harvesting and grading to market.

MISSION AND VISION

Our Mission:

  • Serve the farming community by increasing productivity through maintainence of the soil and plant health.
  • Implement advanced technological innovations.
  • Solve labor crisis by adoptiong and developing new technologies.
  • Provide highly professional agronomy and environmental consulting service.

Our Vision:

To replace traditional agricultural methods into a simple, sustainable and servicable practice.
