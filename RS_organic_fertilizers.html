ORGANIC FERTILIZERS

(Bioaugmented technology driven; NPOP & Vedic organic certified)

Humi Gold-RS

Humi Gold - RS is combination of Organic extracted from Hypnus peat moss and Silica. It is produced in accordance with NOSB (National organic standard board guidelines) It is environmentally safe and not harm full to plant and animals.

Benefits

  • An effective Soil conditioner.
  • A plant growth bio stimulant.
  • A chealating agent and disease supprescent.
  • High in Humic acid, Fulvic acid, Auxin, Vitamins etc.
  • Promote nutrient uptake.
  • Accelerate seed germination.
  • Increase crop yields.

Nutriking-RS

Nutriking -RS is an organic microbial formulation containing strains of bacteria able to synthesize/assimilate atmospheric nitrogen, solubilizes phosphate and potash into available form, thereby supplementing balance nutrition to the crops. It converts non available forms of micro nutrients into available form.

  • Bio-N: 5 X10^9 CFU/gm
  • Bio-P: 5 X10^9CFU/gm
  • Bio-K: 5 X10^9 CFU/gm

Ultra Power-RS

Ultra power - RS is a unique formulation of 90% Water soluble Humic acid, Fulvic acid, Sea weed extract, Amino acids and Enzymes. It can be mixed with water thoroughly to produce liquid formulation, resulting organic formulation compatible with all fertilizers and Micro nutrients.

Benefits

  • Increases the overall growth of the plant.
  • Increases the development of beneficial soil microflora.
  • 20% decrease in the use of chemical fertilizer.
  • Compatible with all organic and inorganic fertilizers.
  • 100% water soluble and residue free product.
  • Improves nutrient uptake.
  • Improves water holding capacity

Power Gel-RS

Power Gel – RS is a organic growth promoter developed with advanced agri microbial technology method. It is enriched with growth hormones and residual free product.

Benefits

  • Increases overall plant growth.
  • Increases yield by 30–40 %.
  • Enrich soil microflora.
  • Enhances nutrient uptake.
  • Enlarges leaf structure.
  • Reduction of pesticide application.

Candid-RS

Candid - RS is a unique formulation of water soluble biological fungicide, composed of beneficial microorganisms like specialized fungi, that are used against fungus that cause plant diseases. It can be mixed with water thoroughly to produce liquid formulation, resulting organic formulation compatible with all fertilizers and Micro nutrients.

Benefits

  • Useful for both soil borne and air born pathogens.
  • Controls almost all kind of fungus and water molds.
  • Compatible with soil drench and foliar spray.
  • Highest retention time on leaf/plant surface.
  • Highly active in all weather conditions.
  • Highly concentrated formulation.

Smaash-RS

Smaash–RS is an effective solution for the management of white-grubs in areca nut, sugarcane, banana, cardamom, groundnut, potato, corn and turf grass. Smaash-RS contains infective juveniles of Entomopathogenic nematodes, Heterorhabditis indica.

Benefits

  • Quick and effective control of root grubs.
  • Safe, environmental and farmer’s friendly.
  • Does not create resistance, resurgence and residues problem.
  • Long lasting pest control.
  • Helps to reduce the pesticide load.
  • Helps to increase the productivity by controlling pests.
