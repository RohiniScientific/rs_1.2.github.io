Sandrain-RS

Sandrain-RS is an unique enzyme formulation with selected strains of Bacilli species. A novel bio augmentation technology for sewage treatment systems. It helps to degrade the sludge in the digestor inturn helps in getting the clear water.

Benefits

  • Eliminates foul odour
  • Reduces sludge in the septic tank.
  • Clear the blockages in sewage pipelines.
  • 100% Organic and Eco-friendly.
  • Keeps pipelines safe.
  • Easy to use.
  • Never stops working.
  • Can withstand toxic shock loads.
  • No more cleaning lorry to clear septic tank.

How to use:

Add Sandrain-RS powder into toilet bowl and flush with enough water.
